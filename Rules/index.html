Rules & Guidelines

Strict enforcement for the most immersive roleplay experience

View Rules Join Discord

Strict Enforcement Policy

We maintain a zero-tolerance policy to ensure immersive, fair, and professional roleplay. All violations are taken seriously with appropriate penalties.

Important Notice

Proper proof is required for all rule violations. Video clips are strongly preferred and provide the most reliable evidence. Witness statements and screenshots may be accepted at staff discretion but are less reliable. False reports will result in penalties.

In-Game Rules

Rule 1 – No Trolling

Permanent Ban
Trolling is intentionally disrupting or ruining roleplay for others. This results in an instant permanent ban. Even minor trolling can ruin immersion, so strict adherence is required.
Proof Required: Clips showing the trolling are preferred. Valid witnesses or photos may be accepted if staff allow.

Rule 2 – No Random Killing (RDM)

1-Hour Kick
Killing other players without roleplay reason is forbidden. This ensures fair gameplay and immersion for everyone.
Proof Required: Clips are preferred. Witnesses or photos are allowed but highly discouraged.

Rule 3 – No Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM)

1-Hour Kick
Intentionally hitting or running over players with vehicles is not allowed. Vehicles exist for realistic transportation, not harming players.
Proof Required: Clips are preferred. Witnesses or photos are secondary.

Rule 4 – No Metagaming

Warning/Kick
Using out-of-character information for in-character actions is prohibited. Metagaming gives unfair advantage and breaks roleplay.
Proof Required: Clips if available. Witnesses or photos are rarely accepted.

Rule 5 – No Powergaming

Warning/Kick
Forcing actions on other players without consent is forbidden. This rule protects fair and realistic roleplay.
Proof Required: Clips are preferred. Witnesses or photos may be accepted at staff discretion.

Rule 6 – Keep Roleplay Realistic

Warning/Kick
All actions must make sense and feel realistic. Silly or impossible actions are punished. Realistic roleplay ensures enjoyment for all players.
Proof Required: Clips are the best proof. Witnesses and photos are secondary.

Rule 7 – Protect Civilians

Warning/Kick
Do not endanger innocent players unnecessarily. Accidents caused by negligence may also be punished. Safety is a priority to maintain immersion.
Proof Required: Clips are preferred. Witnesses or photos are secondary.

Rule 8 – No Exploits or Bugs

Permanent Ban
Using glitches, scripts, or exploits is strictly forbidden. Immediate permanent bans may occur. Fair gameplay must be maintained.
Proof Required: Clips are strongly preferred. Witnesses or photos are only accepted if staff allow.

Rule 9 – Follow Commands

Warning/Kick
Players must follow reasonable in-game commands. Ignoring commands disrupts roleplay. Following commands ensures immersive gameplay for all.
Proof Required: Clips are the most reliable proof, but witnesses or photos may be used if staff approve.

Rule 10 – Respect Property

Warning/Kick
Do not destroy or misuse other players' property without roleplay reason. Violations break immersion and fairness.
Proof Required: Clips are preferred. Witnesses and photos are secondary.

Rule 11 – Consent in Actions

Warning/Kick
Do not force actions on players without consent. Violating this rule disrupts fair roleplay. Respecting other players is essential.
Proof Required: Clips are required if possible. Witnesses and photos may be allowed at staff discretion.

Rule 12 – Drive Responsibly

Warning/Kick
Vehicles must be used safely and realistically. Reckless or unrealistic driving is punishable. Safe driving keeps gameplay immersive.
Proof Required: Clips are preferred. Witnesses or photos may be accepted if staff permit.

Rule 13 – No Abusive Language

Mute/Kick
Harassment or offensive language is forbidden. Respectful communication ensures a positive environment.
Proof Required: Proof is required, ideally clips. Witnesses and photos are secondary.

Rule 14 – No False Reporting

Temporary Kick
Do not submit false reports against players. False witnesses will face temporary kicks to protect fairness.
Proof Required: Proof, preferably clips, is required to validate reports. Witnesses and photos are rarely accepted unless staff approve.

Rule 15 – Repeat Offenses

Escalated Punishments
Breaking rules repeatedly leads to escalated punishments, including kicks or permanent bans. False witnesses will also be penalized.
Proof Required: Clips are preferred for proof. Witnesses and photos are secondary.

Rule 16 – Banned Roleplays

Permanent Ban
  • Sexual / NSFW Roleplay: Any sexualized content, harassment, or sexualization of minors. Always age-appropriate; Roblox strictly forbids this.
  • Real-Life Mass Violence / Terrorism: Extremely traumatic or real-world based violence (unless permitted by ownership).
  • Targeting Real People / Harassment: Threats, bullying, doxxing, or using RP to harass someone outside of the story. Includes hate speech, discrimination, or trolling disguised as RP.

Rule 17 – Follow Roblox & ERLC TOS

Ban & Report
Follow Roblox TOS and ERLC TOS. Not doing so will result in a ban and report to the respective platforms.

Discord Rules

Rule 1 – Zero Tolerance Policy

10-min to Permanent Ban
Los Angeles RP enforces a strict Zero Tolerance Policy on offensive language, slurs, and any words that can be taken as insults, including terms like 'stupid' or 'idiot' and all curse words. Enforcement is automatic through bots and the WIC system. Punishments range from a 10-minute timeout to permanent bans, depending on the severity of the word. Severe cases may be reported to Discord and Emergency Response Liberty County (ERLC). No exceptions are allowed, and staff take this rule extremely seriously.

Rule 2 – No Harassment

Mute/Kick/Ban
Harassment of any kind, including bullying, threats, or targeting members, is strictly prohibited. Violators may be muted, kicked, or banned depending on severity.
Proof Required: Enforcement requires proper proof, ideally clips. Witness statements or screenshots may be accepted if staff allow.

Rule 3 – No Spamming

Mute/Kick
Excessive messages, emojis, or repeated text disrupt chat and are not allowed. Spamming can result in mutes or kicks.
Proof Required: Staff require clips to prove repeated spamming. Screenshots or witness statements may be considered but are less reliable.

Rule 4 – No Advertising

Kick/Ban
Advertising other servers, products, or services is prohibited. Violators may be kicked or banned immediately.
Proof Required: Proper proof, preferably a clip or screenshot, is required. Staff have discretion on enforcement and severity.

Rule 5 – Respect Staff

Warning/Ban
Members must respect all staff members and follow instructions. Disrespect or arguing with staff can result in warnings or bans.
Proof Required: Proof can include clips or screenshots of incidents. Staff may also take witness statements into account.

Rule 6 – No NSFW Content

Instant Ban
Sharing NSFW or inappropriate content is strictly forbidden. Violators may face instant bans.
Proof Required: Proper proof, preferably a clip or screenshot, is required. Staff may exercise discretion if necessary.

Rule 7 – No Racism or Hate Speech

Ban/Kick
Any racist, homophobic, or otherwise discriminatory language will not be tolerated. Violators will face bans or kicks. Maintaining a safe and inclusive environment is our top priority.
Proof Required: Clips are the preferred proof; screenshots or witness statements may be used if staff approve.

Rule 8 – No Threats of DoXXing or Hacking

Permanent Ban
Threats of hacking, doxxing, or revealing personal information are strictly forbidden. Violators may be permanently banned.
Proof Required: Clips showing these threats are preferred for proof. Staff discretion may allow screenshots or witness statements if needed.

Rule 9 – No Excessive Ping Abuse

Mute/Kick
Mass pinging or pinging staff unnecessarily is not allowed. Violators may be muted or kicked.
Proof Required: Clips are preferred to verify the offense. Witnesses or screenshots may be accepted at staff discretion.

Rule 10 – Use Channels Properly

Warning/Mute
Members must post in appropriate channels and follow channel topics. Misuse may result in warnings or mutes. Proper channel usage keeps the server organized.
Proof Required: Clips showing misuse are preferred; screenshots or witness statements may be accepted if staff allow.

Rule 11 – No Impersonation

Instant Ban
Impersonating staff, members, or bots is strictly forbidden. Violators may face instant bans.
Proof Required: Proof is preferred via clips; screenshots or witness statements may be accepted. Staff exercise discretion in enforcement.

Rule 12 – No Malicious Links

Instant Ban
Posting links containing malware, scams, or phishing attempts is strictly forbidden. Violators may be banned immediately. This keeps all members safe online.
Proof Required: Clips showing the link and context are preferred; screenshots or witnesses may be accepted.

Rule 13 – No Spreading Drama

Mute/Kick
Bringing unnecessary drama or conflict into the server is not allowed. Violators may be muted or kicked.
Proof Required: Proof, preferably clips, is required. Witness statements or screenshots may be considered if staff approve.

Rule 14 – English Only

Warning/Mute
All chat must be in English unless stated otherwise. Violations may result in warnings or mutes.
Proof Required: Clips or screenshots are required for proof. Staff discretion applies when enforcing this rule.

Rule 15 – Repeat Offenses

Escalated Punishments
Breaking rules repeatedly leads to escalated punishments including mutes, kicks, or bans. False witnesses will face temporary penalties. Strict enforcement ensures a safe and friendly Discord for all.
Proof Required: Clips are the most reliable proof; witnesses and screenshots are secondary.
