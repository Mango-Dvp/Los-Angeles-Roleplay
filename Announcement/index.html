Zero Tolerance Policy Enforcement Update

October 15, 2025 2:30 PM PST
Urgent

Effective immediately, we are strengthening our Zero Tolerance Policy enforcement. All staff members have been instructed to take immediate action against any form of trolling, harassment, or rule violations without exceptions.

Key changes:

  • Automatic 24-hour ban for first-time RDM/VDM offenses
  • Permanent ban for any form of hate speech or discrimination
  • Increased monitoring of roleplay quality and realism
  • Stricter evidence requirements for appeals

These measures are necessary to maintain the high-quality roleplay environment our community expects. Thank you for your understanding and cooperation.

Server Update v2.1.5 Patch Notes

October 12, 2025 11:45 AM PST
Update

We've just deployed Server Update v2.1.5 with several important fixes and improvements. The server will be more stable and performant with these changes.

Bug Fixes:

  • Fixed vehicle spawning issues in certain districts
  • Resolved texture loading problems for new players
  • Fixed radar system occasionally showing incorrect data
  • Patched exploit allowing unauthorized vehicle modifications

Improvements:

  • Improved server performance during peak hours
  • Enhanced vehicle physics for more realistic driving
  • Updated police database with new vehicle models
  • Optimized emergency response system algorithms

Known Issues:

  • Minor audio sync issues in cutscenes (being investigated)
  • Occasional delay in department radio communications

Please report any new issues to our development team through the appropriate channels.

Important

Available Positions:

  • Administrator (1 position): Manage departments, oversee events, staff coordination

We Need You Program: Livery & Uniform Designers Wanted

October 5, 2025 4:20 PM PST
Important

Hello to all members of the Los Angeles Roleplay community. This is an official and very important announcement from the Los Angeles Roleplay founder. We're not only offering you opportunities but also the chance to make this community way better and to have leadership positions.

Where I Have Been

I have been on a development journey with learning more programs, managing companies as an entrepreneur, and many more personal things and activities.

To be honest with you guys, I have not been focusing on Los Angeles Roleplay, but it's time to change that as I'm not going to let this community fail like I did the others.

What We Need From You

I am a livery designer as well as a developer for Discord, but due to me being very busy and dealing with a lot of personal projects, I need a lot of help.

Before 7 days hits, I'm going to need at least three livery designers and uniform designers immediately due to me not being able to do it currently.

What You Get

Depending on how many liveries you do a month and their quality (whether it's good or bad), you will get a different amount.

Requirements:

  • You must make at least two liveries every 7 days
  • They must reach good quality standards

  • Failure to meet requirements will result in immediate termination

Compensation: 3,000 - 12,000 Robux per month based on quality and quantity of work

Extras
